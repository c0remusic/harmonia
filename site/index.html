Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don’t

A focused, performance-ready harmonic tool — built around a single idea: give you instant access to every valid chord for your key and scale.

♯

Chord Grid

7 diatonic degrees plus a dedicated Borrowed column. All valid chords for your key, always visible — no hidden pages.

↔

Voice Leading

3 algorithms (Anchored, Relative, Piano) with common-tone detection and progressive center pull for natural progressions.

≡

10 Voicings

Classic, Piano, Open, Spread, House, Prog, Rootless A/B, Drop 2, Drop 3 — from tight jazz to wide prog textures.

↻

Live Scale Sync

Instantly import the current key and scale from Ableton Live — stay in sync with your session at all times.

♭

Borrowed Chords

Secondary dominants and modal interchange chords right in the main grid — always one click away.

◉

MIDI & Hold

Full keyboard mapping, clickable grid and Hold/Latch mode for performance — with visual keyboard feedback.
