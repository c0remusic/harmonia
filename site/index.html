Tuple

A harmonic composition tool for fast and musical chord selection, exploration and performance — directly inside Ableton Live.

Download ▶︎ Try it Features
tuple — interactive demo

Ableton Live

Built right into
your session

Tuple isn’t a standalone app — it’s a Max for Live device that lives on a MIDI track. It reads your project, plays your instruments and follows you while you write.

Native Max for Live

Drop tuple.amxd onto a MIDI track — no standalone app, no extra setup. It runs inside Live like any stock device.

Plays your instruments

Tuple streams MIDI to whatever instrument follows it — synths, samplers, Rhodes, anything. Your bass keeps its own track and its own sound.

Live scale sync

One click imports Live’s current key and scale, so the chord grid always matches your session — no manual setup.

Made for performance

Hold/Latch, a clickable grid and full keyboard mapping for playing live — with Push 2 pad integration on the way.

Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don’t

A focused, performance-ready harmonic tool — built around a single idea: give you instant access to every valid chord for your key and scale.

Chord Grid

7 diatonic degrees plus a dedicated Borrowed column. All valid chords for your key, always visible — no hidden pages.

Voice Leading

3 algorithms (Anchored, Relative, Piano) with common-tone detection and progressive center pull for natural progressions.

15 Voicings

Classic, Piano, Open, Spread, House, Prog, Rootless A/B, Drop 2/3, Jazz, Nu-House, Trap, Trance, Funk — from tight jazz to wide electronic textures.

One-hand voicings

Each voicing is a coherent grip in a single register, ready to layer over your own bass line — only Piano mode spans two hands.

Borrowed Chords

Secondary dominants and modal interchange chords right in the main grid — always one click away.

Color-coded degrees

Five color logics — spectrum, function, tension, fifths, quality — make the whole grid readable at a glance.
