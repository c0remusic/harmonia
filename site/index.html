Harmonia

A harmonic composition tool for fast and musical chord selection, exploration, and performance - directly inside Ableton Live.

Features

Harmonia is not a sequencer. It's a focused, performance-ready harmonic tool - built around a single idea: give you instant access to every valid chord for your key and scale.

Chord Grid

7 diatonic degrees + a dedicated Borrowed column. All valid chords for your key, always visible.

Voice Leading

3 algorithms (Anchored, Relative, Piano) with common tone detection for natural progressions.

10 Voicings

Classic, Piano, Open, Spread, House, Prog, Rootless A/B, Drop2, Drop3 - from tight jazz voicings to wide prog textures.

Live Scale Sync

Instantly import the current key and scale from Ableton Live - stay in sync with your session.

Borrowed Chords

Secondary dominants and modal interchange chords in the main grid - always one click away.

MIDI and Hold

Full keyboard mapping, clickable grid, Hold/Latch mode for performance - plus visual keyboard feedback.
